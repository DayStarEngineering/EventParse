\subsection{Detailed Description}
Stores a polynomial. 

Here, a polynomial is defined as a finite series of the form \[ a_0 + a_1 x + a_2 x^2 + \cdots + a_N x^N, \] where $ a_k $, for $k=0,\ldots,N$, are real coefficients, and $N$ is the degree of the polynomial.
